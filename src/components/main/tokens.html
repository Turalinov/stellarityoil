Meet our tokens

  • Token name: CrudeOil
  • Symbol: OILT
  • Total supply: 1 Mil

CrudeOil provide an opportunity to invest in both the oil market and cryptocurrency. OILT tokens shall be available for purchase in all its phases. Each OILT token represents an oil contract, where OilSupply commits to repurchase it in exchange for 1 gallon of Giddings crude oil, or its equivalent market price, at a maturity date.
